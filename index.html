Online Shopper with Color Blindness

Lee: User with Color Blindness

Description: Lee is colorblind and encounters barriers when shopping online. He has difficulty distinguishing red and green colors, which appear as shades of brown to him.

Challenges Faced:

  • Low color contrast in text and images.
  • Use of color alone to indicate required fields and sale prices.
  • Difficulty distinguishing color swatches that do not have labels with the color names.

Solutions that Help Lee:

  • Proper color contrast with adjustable settings.
  • Use of asterisks instead of color alone to indicate required fields.
  • Color swatches labeled with text (e.g., "Red," "Green," etc.) for easier identification.
